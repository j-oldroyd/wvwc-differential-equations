For all \(x\text{,}\) the following equations hold:
\begin{align*} e^{ix} & = \cos x + i\sin x \\ \cos x & = \frac{e^{ix}+e^{-ix}}{2} \\ \sin x & = \frac{e^{ix} - e^{-ix}}{2i} \text{.} \end{align*}
